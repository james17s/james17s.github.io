Mobile Legends Heroes

Belerick

Belerick releases vines in a designated direction, dealing 200(+50%Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies on the path and slow them down by 25%. Meanwhile, he leaves Ancient Seed on the path, and after 1s, these seeds will deal 200(+50%Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies on the path and taunt them for 1.2s.

Vexana

Vexana marks the target area with the power of the undead, slowing enemies within by 30%. After a 1s delay, a blast of Nether Energy will strike at the area, dealing 450(+200%Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies hit.

Sun

Sun, the immortal mountain spirit born from a magical stone, roamed freely between heaven and earth until one day, when the cries of a group of pandas caught his attention. It was from them that he learned the existence of death.

Gusion

Gusion was the first hero to deal both physical and magic damage on skills. (Physical: passive skill; Magic: all active skills) His revamp made his passive deal magic damage. Gusion is the first and only hero whose active skills have second phases.
